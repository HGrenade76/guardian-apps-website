How DECISIO Works

Prepare. Decide. Act.
Follow through.

Most meeting apps stop at the transcript. DECISIO.ai does the part that actually moves work forward.

01 Prepare

Walk in knowing where things left off.

Tap to generate a pre-meeting brief from your history with a person or team — open actions, prior decisions, and what to follow up on.

02 Decide

A decision gets made. It doesn't get lost.

Decisions are captured automatically and logged — searchable months later, and ready to reverse, supersede, or revisit.

03 Act

An action no one owns is just a hope.

Every action item gets an owner, a date, and a status in the Action Register. Open, due soon, or overdue — at a glance.

04 Follow Through

Leaders make decisions findable.

The Weekly Brief surfaces your one thing first on Monday morning, with an executive summary and a shareable PDF report.
