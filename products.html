Decision & Follow-Through · iOS

DECISIO.ai

Turn every meeting into structured decisions, tracked actions, and executive-ready briefs. Audio stays on your device; cloud AI runs on your own keys. No bots. No compromise.

  • One-tap recording with live waveform — no bots
  • On-device transcription with speaker recognition
  • Automatic decision & action extraction
  • Action Register with owners, deadlines & statuses
  • Pre-meeting prep & Weekly Executive Briefs
  • iCloud sync · Face ID · Calendar · Siri & Shortcuts

Real screens. Real outcomes.

Meeting Prep

Walk in with the agenda already built.

Plan around the person and DECISIO assembles your open items, decisions to make, and risks from past sessions — then lets you email it or drop it on the calendar.

Ask My Work

Question months of work in plain English.

Search across every meeting, decision, and action — or just ask. "What are my overdue items?" "What did we decide last week?" Real answers, instantly.

Weekly Brief

Every Monday, know exactly where things stand.

A look-back on decisions made, commitments kept, what's overdue, and your momentum for the week — so nothing slips before anyone has to ask.
