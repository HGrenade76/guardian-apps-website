How It Works

From site to report
in four steps.

01

Capture On Site

Use the guided workflow to capture conditions, photos, and observations in real time — while you're still on the ground.

02

AI Analyzes Hazards

The AI reviews your captured data and photos, identifies hazards, classifies risk, and surfaces recommended actions.

03

Review & Finalize

Review AI-generated findings, add your professional judgment, and finalize the inspection record on your device.

04

Share Instantly

Generate a polished PDF report and share it with your team, your customer, or leadership — in minutes, not days.
