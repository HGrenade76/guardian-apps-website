Artificial Intelligence (AI) Processing

5.1 How AI Features Work

The App incorporates artificial-intelligence features that generate automated insights from content you submit. When you invoke an AI feature, selected images, text, or audio may be transmitted to third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI) solely for the purpose of producing results for you.

5.2 AI Accuracy Disclaimer

AI-generated results are NOT guaranteed to be accurate, complete, current, or error-free. AI outputs do NOT constitute professional, legal, safety, medical, engineering, regulatory, or any other form of expert advice. AI results may contain biases, hallucinations, omissions, or inaccuracies inherent in machine-learning systems.

5.3 User Responsibilities

You are solely responsible for independently reviewing, verifying, and validating all AI-generated outputs before relying on them; making all final decisions based on your own professional judgment; ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and regulations; and obtaining independent professional advice where necessary.

Guardian Apps LLC provides AI features strictly "as is" and "as available," and assumes no responsibility or liability for any actions taken or decisions made in reliance on AI-generated content.

5.4 AI Data Retention by Third Parties

By default, third-party AI providers may retain API inputs and outputs for limited periods (e.g., up to 30 days) solely for security monitoring and abuse prevention. Where technically available and commercially feasible, we enable zero-data-retention configurations. We do not use your data to train AI models.

5.5 Automated Decision-Making (GDPR Art. 22)

The App does not make decisions based solely on automated processing that produce legal effects or significantly affect you. AI features are tools that provide suggestions; all final decisions remain yours.
