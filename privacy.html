Overview

About This Policy

Guardian Apps LLC ("we," "us," "our") operates the Guardian Apps mobile applications and associated websites, digital services, and companion applications available through the Apple App Store and other distribution platforms (collectively, the "Services").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information. It is designed to comply with applicable data-protection laws worldwide, including EU GDPR, UK-GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, PIPEDA, LGPD, and other applicable frameworks.

By downloading, installing, accessing, or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. Where required by law, your continued use constitutes consent to the practices described herein.

Section 2

Categories of Personal Data We Collect

2.1 Account and Profile Data

  • Name, email address, and account credentials
  • Optional profile logo, avatar, or signature
  • User preferences and settings

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Inspection data, customer and site details, and notes
  • Photographs, images, and files you capture, upload, or create
  • Voice and audio recordings (only when you explicitly enable recording features)
  • Text, annotations, and other content you input

2.3 Location Data

Approximate or precise geolocation is collected only with your explicit, affirmative consent and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

2.4 Device and Technical Identifiers

  • App-generated unique identifiers
  • Device type, operating system, and version information
  • IP address (collected automatically by servers processing your requests)

2.5 Purchase and Subscription Data

In-app purchase and subscription history is processed by the respective app store. We do not receive or store payment card information or bank account details.

2.6 Usage and Diagnostics Data

  • Feature usage analytics
  • Crash logs, error reports, and performance diagnostics
  • Session duration and frequency of use

2.7 AI Analysis Data

Images, text, audio, or other content you voluntarily submit for AI-powered analysis, and AI-generated outputs derived from your submissions.

Section 5

Artificial Intelligence Processing

5.1 How AI Features Work

The App incorporates AI features that generate automated insights from content you submit. When you invoke an AI feature, selected images, text, or audio may be transmitted to third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI) solely for the purpose of producing results for you.

5.2 AI Accuracy Disclaimer

AI outputs are NOT guaranteed to be accurate, complete, or error-free. AI outputs do NOT constitute professional, legal, safety, medical, engineering, or regulatory advice. AI results may contain biases, hallucinations, omissions, or inaccuracies inherent in machine-learning systems.

5.3 AI Data Retention

Third-party AI providers may retain API inputs and outputs for limited periods (e.g., up to 30 days) solely for security monitoring. Where available, we enable zero-data-retention configurations. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Section 6

Voice and Audio Recording

You must obtain all required permissions and consents from individuals being recorded before initiating any recording. You are solely responsible for complying with all applicable recording and consent laws in your jurisdiction. Audio data is processed solely to provide the functionality you have requested.

Section 7

Data Storage and Security

  • On-Device Storage: Drafts, media, and local data are stored on your device under your device's security controls
  • iCloud Sync: When enabled, data syncs to your Apple iCloud account, subject to Apple's security and privacy policies
  • Data in transit is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS)
  • Data at rest uses platform-provided encryption (iOS Data Protection)

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials.

Section 8

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for monetary or other valuable consideration.

We may share personal data with Apple Inc. (for app-store and iCloud services), AI service providers (limited to content you submit for analysis), cloud infrastructure providers, analytics providers (aggregated/de-identified data only), and legal/regulatory authorities when required by law.

Section 9

International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States. Where data is transferred outside the EEA or UK, we rely on European Commission adequacy decisions, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), or other appropriate safeguards.

Section 11

Your Privacy Rights

EU GDPR / UK-GDPR Rights

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): Obtain confirmation and a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to Know, Delete, Correct, and Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing
  • Right to Non-Discrimination for exercising your privacy rights
  • We do not sell or share data for cross-context behavioral advertising

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@myguardianapps.com. We aim to respond within 30 days.

Section 12

Device Permissions

The App may request access to: Camera, Photo Library, Files, Microphone, Location Services, Notifications, and Bluetooth. Each permission is requested only when the associated feature is accessed and can be revoked at any time in your device settings.

Section 13

Subscriptions and Billing

  • All billing, payment processing, and refunds are handled exclusively by the Apple App Store
  • Guardian Apps LLC does not collect, process, or store payment card or bank account information
  • Refunds and payment disputes must be directed to Apple directly
  • Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID account settings
Section 15

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (U.S.) or under 16 (EEA/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at support@myguardianapps.com and we will delete such information promptly.
