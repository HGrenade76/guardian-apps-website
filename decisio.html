How It Works

Six capabilities.
One complete system.

🎙️

Record & Transcribe

One-tap recording captures high-quality audio. AI transcription with speaker recognition means you always know who said what — and who committed to what.

🧠

AI Decision Extraction

DECISIO.ai doesn't just transcribe — it thinks. The AI automatically identifies decisions made, action items with owners, risks flagged, and open questions needing follow-up.

📋

Decision Log

Every decision from every meeting, searchable and organized. Build an institutional record of what was decided, when, and in which meeting. No more "I thought we agreed to..."

✅

Action Register

Track every action item across all your meetings in one place. See what's due, what's overdue, and who owns it. Filter by owner, priority, status, or meeting.

📊

Executive Briefings

Generate polished PDF reports with one tap. Share executive summaries or full transcripts. Weekly briefs give a consolidated view of all decisions and actions across meetings.

🔒

Built for Privacy

Recordings sync securely via iCloud. Face ID and passcode protection keep sensitive content secure. Confidentiality levels let you classify meetings appropriately.

Your meetings.
