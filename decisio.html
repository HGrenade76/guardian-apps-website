Why this exists

A decision gets made. Everyone nods. Then — nothing.

No clear owner. No follow-through. No memory of what you decided. By Monday, the meeting that felt productive has produced nothing. The problem was never the meeting. It's everything after it — the part no tool was built to hold. That's the part DECISIO.ai runs.

Roughly 70% of what's said is forgotten within a day without review. The forgetting curve — Ebbinghaus, 1885; replicated 2015.
How it works

Three moves. Prepare. Decide. Follow through.

Each move is a real screen you just scrolled through — not a promise on a landing page.

01
Prepare

Walk in knowing exactly what this meeting is for.

A meeting without an agenda is a meeting without a point. Plan around the person, and DECISIO pulls your open items, decisions to make, and risks from past sessions — then drafts the agenda for you.

  • Prep by person — your history together, surfaced before you walk in
  • Send the agenda — email it or drop it on the calendar in one tap
02
Decide

Every decision captured, the moment it's made.

Record one-tap, and the AI lifts each decision out of the conversation into a searchable Decision Log — with the meeting it came from, the actions it spawned, and whether it still stands.

  • AI decision extraction — decisions, owners, risks, open questions
  • Ask My Work — question months of meetings in plain English
03
Follow through

They remember what you followed up on.

An action no one owns isn't an action — it's a hope. The Action Register gives every commitment an owner, a date, and a status, and the Weekly Brief surfaces what's overdue before anyone has to ask.

  • Action Register — owners, deadlines, and status across every meeting
  • Weekly Brief — your week's wins, overdue items, and momentum

Privacy first

Your meetings. Your data.

Built around one principle: your conversations belong to you. Here's exactly what stays on your device — and the one thing that leaves it, and why.

  • Your audio and voice never leave your deviceRecording and transcription happen on your iPhone using Apple's on-device speech framework. The audio — and your voiceprint — are never uploaded.
  • AI analysis works from text onlyTo extract decisions, actions, and briefs, only the text transcript is sent to an AI provider, on your own keys. Never your audio. Never your voice.
  • No meeting botsNo third-party bot joins your calls. Nothing for anyone else to install or approve.
  • Locked behind Face IDSensitive content is protected by Face ID, passcode, and iOS Data Protection. iCloud sync runs through your own Apple account.
