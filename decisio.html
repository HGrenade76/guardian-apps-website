How It Works

Six capabilities.
One follow-through system.

🎙️

Record & Transcribe

One-tap recording with a live waveform. Transcription runs on your device with speaker recognition, so you always know who said what — and who committed to what.

🧠

AI Decision Extraction

DECISIO.ai doesn't just transcribe — it reads the room. It surfaces decisions made, action items with owners, risks flagged, and open questions that still need follow-up.

📋

Pre-Meeting Briefs Pro

Tap to generate a brief from your past conversations with a person or team. Walk in knowing exactly where things left off — open actions, prior decisions, and what to follow up on.

✅

Action Register

Every action item across every meeting in one place, each with an owner, a date, and a status. Open, in progress, due soon, or overdue — visible at a glance, nothing buried in a thread.

🗂️

Decision Log

Every decision, searchable across all sessions. Months later, "what did we decide about Q3?" takes seconds — with the related actions tied to it. Reverse, supersede, or revisit any call.

📊

Weekly Brief Pro

Monday morning, one screen. The Monday Mandate surfaces your one thing first, plus an executive summary and a PDF report with an "At a Glance" band and a "Bottom Line" callout up front.

Privacy First

Your meetings.
Your data.

DECISIO.ai was built around one principle: your conversations belong to you. Recorded on your device, analyzed on your terms — your meeting content never touches Guardian's servers.

  • Your audio never leaves your deviceRecording and transcription run on your iPhone using Apple's on-device speech framework. Audio and voice stay local — never uploaded.
  • Cloud AI runs on your own keysWhen AI analysis uses the cloud, it runs through your own AI provider keys — never ours. No meeting content sits on infrastructure you don't control.
  • No meeting botsNo third-party bot joins your calls. No permissions granted to outside services.
  • Face ID & iOS Data ProtectionSensitive meeting content is protected by Face ID, your passcode, and iOS Data Protection.
